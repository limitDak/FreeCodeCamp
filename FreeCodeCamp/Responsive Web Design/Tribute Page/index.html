Asta

Savior of the Clover Kingdom

Asta's Achievements

Despite being in the Magic Knights for only a little over a year, Asta has become one of the most notable Magic Knights in the kingdom. He was chosen alongside with his childhood rival Yuno as the Top Rookies during the Star Festival and were given high praise from the current Wizard King, Julius Novachrono. Below are just a few of his most notable achievements.

  • Protected Saussy Village from an attack by the Eye of the Midnight Sun.
  • Defended the Underwater Temple from one of the Third Eyes from the Eye of the Midnight Sun.
  • Fought against the Diamond Kingdom and one of the Third Eyes from the Eye of the Midnight Sun during the invasion of the Witch's Forest.
  • Aided the Black Bulls in acquiring the Magic Stones.
  • Dealt the final blow to Zagred, the devil mastermind behind the reincarnation of the elves and the destruction of the Clover Kingdom.
